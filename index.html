How about yokosuka city

横須賀市は神奈川県南部に位置し、三浦半島の中心に広がる海と歴史の街。
日本の海軍文化の拠点であり、外国文化が混ざり合ったユニークな雰囲気が特徴です。
ドブ板通りや観音崎、猿島など観光名所も豊富です。

Yokosuka City is located in the southern part of Kanagawa Prefecture, a city of sea and history spread across the center of the Miura Peninsula.
It is a hub of Japanese naval culture and features a unique atmosphere blending foreign cultures.
There are many tourist attractions such as Dobuita Street, Kannonzaki, and Sarushima.
